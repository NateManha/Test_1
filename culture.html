Culture

Streets

Granada is a very walkable city
with little cafes and restaurants around every corner.
Some of the main attractions are:

  • Mountain climbing
  • Explore churches and catherdrals
  • Mountain bike tours

People

The people of Granada are very welcoming, inviting,
and love a good time,
listed below are some of the common things they do:

  • Tapas Hopping
  • Enjoy the night life
  • Watch Football

Architecture

The architecture of Granada is the city's most stunning feature, these amazing structures date back to the 1400s
when the Arabs used them as a stronghold against foreign entities. listed below are the most famous buildings.

  • Alhambra
  • Generalife
  • Mirador de san Nicolas
  • Albayzin
  • Royal Chapel of Granada

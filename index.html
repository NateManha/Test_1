Granada

Spain

Description

Granada, Spain. A city that offers the aspects of the modern world while still holding onto the classic Moorish architecture. A palace named the Alhambra is the most famous example of Moorish design. This stunning city is located in the Andalusia region at the base of the Sierra Nevada mountain range. The city's symbol is the pomegranate, which is interesting because the rough translation of the word Granada is pomegranate. Granada has always been a famous and important part of Spain's history.

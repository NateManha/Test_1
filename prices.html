Flights

Flights to Granada are not crazy expensive,
depending on the season and the month flights can range from $150-$750 round trip.
Some of the most popular airlines that fly to Granada are:

  • American Airlines
  • Iberian Airways

Restaurants

Food is always one of the main things i think about when traveling,
Without knowing where the good restaurants are,
you will end up at Mcdonalds because they are everywhere and it is a safe choice.
Before I go traveling I always look up 3-5 restaurants that look good and work it into my budget
so I can enjoy being there and not worry about money. I have found that by doing this I enjoy my trips much more.
Below are some of my favorite restaurants that I would like visit while in Granada.

  • La Telefonica
  • Restaurante Cala
  • Biloba
  • Tocateja
  • Entrebrasas
  • El Cambalache
